Welcome to Namoroka

Welcome to Namoroka! Namoroka aims to bring back the beloved design of the classic Firefox 1-3 interface. Click Next to continue the setup wizard.
You can find more options on "Tools > Namoroka Options" in the menu bar after finishing the wizard.

Completing the Namoroka Wizard

Thank you for choosing Namoroka! It is recommended to restart the browser to apply all changes. You can also customize the theme further in the Namoroka Options.
